Realme C65 Review

Pros:

  • Large 6.67-inch IPS LCD display
  • Powerful Mediatek MT6769Z Helio G85 chipset
  • 8GB of RAM for smooth performance
  • 5000mAh battery with fast charging support
  • Quad-camera setup with 50MP primary sensor

Cons:

  • No AMOLED display
  • No 5G connectivity
  • No high refresh rate
  • Plastic build quality
  • No IP rating for dust and water resistance

Verdict:

The Realme C65 is a solid mid-range smartphone that offers excellent performance, a large display, and a long-lasting battery. However, it lacks some premium features like AMOLED display, 5G connectivity, and high refresh rate. Overall, it's a great value for money device for those who want a reliable smartphone without breaking the bank.
