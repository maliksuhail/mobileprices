Xiaomi Redmi 13

Pros:

1. Impressive Camera Performance: The Redmi 13 boasts a 108MP main shooter, which can capture decent photos. However, there are some limitations in video capture (limited to 1080p@30fps, no EIS, and mono audio).
2. Good Gaming Performance: The device offers satisfactory gaming performance.
. Big Battery with Fast Charging: The 5,000mAh battery supports 33W fast charging.
4. Dust and Splash Resistant: The Redmi 13 has basic IP53 ingress protection .

Cons:

1. No 2K Video Recording: Unfortunately, the Redmi 13 lacks 2K video recording capability.
2. Not Bright Enough Outdoors: The display isn't very bright for comfortable outdoor use and lacks HDR video support.
3. Single Loudspeaker: The loudspeaker quality is not particularly impressive.
4. Virtual Gyroscope and Proximity Sensor: Some essential sensors are missing.
5. Inadequate Performance: The MediaTek Helio G91 Ultra and eMMC storage may lead to frequent stutters and slowdowns.

Verdict:

The Redmi 13 looks promising on paper, especially with its high-resolution camera. However, it's essential to consider its limitations, such as video recording quality and overall performance. While it's competitively priced, there are other viable options in the market, like the OnePlus Nord CE 3 Lite and Realme C55, which offer better features at similar price points.
