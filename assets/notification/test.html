Tecno Spark 20 Pro

Pros:

1. Impressive Battery Life: The Spark 20 Pro boasts a large 5000 mAh battery, providing extended usage without frequent charging.
2. Vibrant Display: The 6.78-inch IPS LCD screen with a 120Hz refresh rate offers vivid colors and smooth visuals.
3. Decent Camera Performance: The 108 MP primary camera captures detailed photos, and the 32 MP front camera is suitable for selfies.
4. Smooth Day-to-Day Functionality: The octa-core processor and 8 GB RAM ensure smooth multitasking and app performance.
5. Affordable Price Point: It delivers good value for users seeking a reliable device without breaking the bank.

Cons:

1. Not High-End: While it performs well for its price, it doesn't match flagship devices in terms of camera quality or processing power.

Verdict:

The Tecno Spark 20 Pro offers a well-rounded experience at an affordable price. It's a great choice for everyday use, especially if you prioritize battery life and display quality. However, if you need cutting-edge features or 5G support, you might want to explore higher-end options .
