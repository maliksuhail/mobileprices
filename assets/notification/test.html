Infinix Smart 8 Plus

Pros:

1. Big Display with 90Hz Refresh Rate: The Smart 8 Plus features a large 6.6-inch IPS LCD display with a smooth 90Hz refresh rate, providing an immersive viewing experience.
2. Good Camera Performance: The dual-camera setup (50 MP + 0.08 MP) captures decent photos, especially in daylight conditions.
3. Magic Ring Capsule: The device includes a unique Magic Ring capsule for additional functionality.
4. Ring-LED Flash Support: The camera setup is equipped with a ring-LED flash for better low-light photography.
5. Huge Battery with 18W Fast Charging: The 5000mAh battery ensures long-lasting usage, and the 18W fast charging feature keeps you powered up quickly.

Cons:

1. Camera Exposure Issue: Some users have reported exposure issues with the camera.
2. No NFC: Unfortunately, the Smart 8 Plus lacks NFC support.
Verdict
The Infinix Smart 8 Plus offers a large display, good camera performance, and impressive battery life. However, the absence of NFC and occasional camera exposure issues might be drawbacks for some users. Overall, it's a pocket-friendly device with notable features .
