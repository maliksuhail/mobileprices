Tecno Spark 20 Pro

Pros:

- Design: The Spark 20 Pro+ boasts an eye-catching design with a faux leather back, giving it a premium feel.
- Display: The curved AMOLED display with thin bezels looks great and competently handles its 120Hz high refresh rate.
- Storage: It comes with 8GB of RAM and 256GB of storage, which is impressive for a budget device.
- Ingress Protection: You get decent IP53 ingress protection and Gorilla Glass 5.
- Battery: The 5,000 mAh battery provides good endurance.

Cons:

- Video Capture: Video capture is limited to 1080p.
- Stereo Speakers: There are no stereo speakers.

Verdict:

The Tecno Spark 20 Pro+ is a surprisingly well-rounded device for its very budget-friendly price point. It delivers a solid experience with minimal compromises. If you're looking for a reliable smartphone without breaking the bank, the Spark 20 Pro+ is definitely worth considering .
Overall, it competes well in its segment, offering competitive pricing, decent performance, and reliable camera capabilities.
